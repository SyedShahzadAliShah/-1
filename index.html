About Us

Our Legacy of Learning

A cornerstone of public higher education in East Karachi since 1975.

Est. 1975 · Model Colony

Committed to Excellence in Higher Education

Allama Iqbal Government Degree College-2, located in the heart of Model Colony, Karachi, has been a beacon of affordable, quality education for nearly five decades. Affiliated with the University of Karachi, the college serves thousands of students from diverse backgrounds.

Our mission is to provide accessible higher education that empowers students with knowledge, critical thinking, and ethical values — preparing them to contribute meaningfully to society and the nation.
